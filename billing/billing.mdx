# Managing Your Billing

This page gives you a clear overview of your service's billing and subscription status through your XGamingServer control panel.

## Understanding Your Plan

At the top of the billing page, you'll see a comprehensive summary of your current plan:

### Plan Details
- **Product Name** - The specific server package you purchased
- **Price & Billing Cycle** - Your monthly or annual cost
- **Current Status** - Active, Suspended, Cancelled, etc.
- **Next Payment Date** - When your next payment is due
- **Server Resources** - RAM, CPU cores, storage allocation

### Service Information
- **Server Location** - Data center where your server is hosted
- **IP Address** - Your server's connection details
- **Activation Date** - When your service began
- **Contract Duration** - Length of your current billing period

## Important Billing Alerts

The panel will display critical warnings about your billing status:

<Warning>
**Suspended Service**: If your service is suspended for non-payment, a red warning will appear with a "Pay Now" button. Your server will be inaccessible until payment is received.
</Warning>

<Info>
**Cancellation Notice**: If you've requested to cancel your service, an orange notification will confirm the termination date. Your server will remain active until that date.
</Info>

<Tip>
**Payment Reminder**: You'll receive email notifications 7, 3, and 1 day before your payment is due.
</Tip>

## Available Billing Actions

### Change Plan
Modify your server specifications:
- **Upgrade** - Increase RAM, CPU, or storage
- **Downgrade** - Reduce resources to lower costs
- **Switch Locations** - Move to a different data center

<Note>
Plan changes typically take effect immediately, with prorated billing adjustments.
</Note>

### Manage Service
Access the complete billing portal to:
- View and download invoices
- Update payment methods
- Modify billing address and contact information
- View payment history
- Set up automatic payments

### Cancel Service
Request service cancellation with options:
- **Immediate Cancellation** - Stop service and billing right away
- **End of Period** - Cancel at the end of your current billing cycle
- **Pause Service** - Temporarily suspend (where available)

## Payment Methods

### Accepted Payment Types
- Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Bank Transfer (for annual plans)
- Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum) - select plans

### Payment Security
- All transactions are encrypted with SSL
- We never store full credit card numbers
- Payment processing handled by secure third-party providers
