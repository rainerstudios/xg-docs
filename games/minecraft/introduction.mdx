## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="What are the specs on the servers?">
Our servers use the most powerful hardware on the market, solely using NVMe SSDs and a minimum of 1Gbps network port for every server.

- CPU: **Ryzen 9 5950X** or better (Plus), **Ryzen 9 7950X** or better (Elder)
- Gen 3 and 4 NVMe SSDs with RAID 1 configuration
- **DDR4** RAM (Plus), **DDR5 ECC** RAM (Elder)
- **1Gbps** Network Port (Plus), **10Gbps** Network Port (Elder)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does setup take?">
Servers are setup instantly, allowing you to create your Minecraft server within minutes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What locations are the servers in?">
**Dallas, Texas** (Central USA), **Spokane, Washington** (West USA),**Ashburn, Virginia** (East USA), **London, United Kingdom** (EU), **Germany** (EU), and **Amsterdam** (EU).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I migrate my existing server to BerryByte?">
Yes! We offer free server migrations from your old host and help you get settled into BerryByte!
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you support mods/modpacks or other server types?">
For sure! You are welcome to host whichever modpack you'd like, from RLCraft, ATM8, BetterMC, FTB, Enigmatica 2, and more! Make sure to select either **Forge** or **Fabric** during checkout.

To upload and use mods, simply drag the mod into the `/mods` folder in the file manager and restart your server.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I upgrade/downgrade my server later on?">
Absolutely! You can upgrade and downgrade plans at any time with a few click of a button! You can also migrate between lineups too! (e.g Minecraft Standard to Minecraft Elder and vice-versa)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you support MySQL databases?">
We do! All of our Minecraft servers have **5 database slots** included which can be **raised for free**! (as long as the databases are being appropriately used for Minecraft).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are the servers DDoS protected?">
Protecting your servers against DDoS attacks and bad actors are our specialty! Terabit-scale game DDoS protection is **included with every server** at no extra cost.
</Accordion>
