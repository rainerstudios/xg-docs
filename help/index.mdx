## Billing Information

### Payment Methods

We accept multiple payment options for your convenience:

**Credit & Debit Cards**
- Powered by Stripe for secure processing
- Supports Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- Automatic billing for subscriptions

**PayPal**
- Secure online payments
- One-click checkout for returning customers
- PayPal balance or linked accounts

**SEPA Bank Transfers**
- Direct bank transfers for European customers
- Powered by Stripe
- Ideal for recurring payments

**Cryptocurrency**
- Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major cryptocurrencies
- Powered by Coinbase Commerce
- Perfect for privacy-conscious users

### Billing Cycles

**Monthly Billing**
- Charged every 30 days from activation
- Pro-rated adjustments for upgrades/downgrades
- Cancel anytime with immediate or end-of-cycle options

**Annual Billing**
- Save money with yearly subscriptions
- Discounted rates compared to monthly
- Full refund available within 48 hours

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="How do I change my payment method?">
Login to your [billing portal](https://billing.xgamingserver.com), select your service, and update your payment method in the billing section. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I pause my subscription temporarily?">
We don't offer subscription pausing, but you can cancel and restart anytime. Your server data is preserved for 7 days after cancellation, allowing easy reactivation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if my payment fails?">
We'll attempt to charge your payment method 3 times over 7 days. If unsuccessful, your server will be suspended until payment is resolved. Contact support for assistance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for multiple servers?">
Yes! Contact our sales team for volume discounts on multiple servers or long-term commitments. We offer competitive pricing for communities and organizations.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I download my server data before canceling?">
Use the backup feature in your game panel to create a full server backup, then download it to your computer. This ensures you don't lose any progress or configurations.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
